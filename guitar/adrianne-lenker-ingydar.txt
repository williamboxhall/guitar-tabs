This is a weird tuning, kinda of an open C# but with the 5th string tuned up one step
(C#-G#-C#-F-A#-C#) (Drop-D tuned down a step)

The highest three strings are played open, in triplets, with index, middle and ring fingers throughout.
Meanwhile, the thumb plays the bass notes like a metronome.
It's hard at first to make the two parts work together, but when you master it it gives a mesmerizing effect.
